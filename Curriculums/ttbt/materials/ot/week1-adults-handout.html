Through The Bible Together

Week 1: Creation and the Fall

Old Testament Session
Adults (18+)

This Week's Focus

Journey through God's perfect creation, the first humans made in His image, and the devastating effects of sin entering the world. Discover how even in judgment, God shows mercy and promises hope.

Memory Verses

"In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth."

Genesis 1:1

"So God created man in His own image; in the image of God He created him; male and female He created them."

Genesis 1:27
Berean Standard Bible

Day 1: Creation

Today's Reading

Genesis 1:1 - 2:3

Deeper Dive

  • What does it mean that God created everything "good"? How does this shape our understanding of the physical world?
  • How does being made "in God's image" make humans special? What responsibilities does this bring?
  • Why did God rest on the seventh day? What does this teach us about work and rest?
  • How does the order of creation (light, separation, life) reflect God's character and purposes?

Day 2: Adam and Eve

Today's Reading

Genesis 2:4-24

Deeper Dive

  • What was Adam's job in the Garden of Eden? How does work fit into God's design for humanity?
  • Why did God say "it is not good for man to be alone"? What does this reveal about human nature?
  • What does "one flesh" mean in marriage? How does this reflect God's design for relationships?
  • How does the intimacy without shame (v.25) contrast with our experience today?

Day 3: The Fall

Today's Reading

Genesis 3

Deeper Dive

  • How did the serpent tempt Eve to disobey God? What tactics does Satan still use today?
  • What were the consequences of Adam and Eve's sin for themselves, their relationship, and creation?
  • How did God show mercy even in His judgment? What hope is found in Genesis 3:15?
  • How does the shame and hiding in this chapter contrast with the openness of chapter 2?

Day 4: Cain and Abel

Today's Reading

Genesis 4

Deeper Dive

  • Why did God accept Abel's offering but not Cain's? What does this teach about worship and the heart?
  • How did sin spread from Adam and Eve to their children? What does this reveal about human nature?
  • What does it mean to be your "brother's keeper"? How does this apply to our relationships today?
  • How does God's protection of Cain show His mercy even toward the guilty?

This Week's Big Ideas

Creation: God made everything perfect and declared it "very good."
Image of God: Humans are uniquely created to reflect God's character and rule over creation.
The Fall: Sin broke our relationship with God and brought death and corruption into the world.
Hope: Even in judgment, God shows mercy and promises a way back to Him through the coming Seed.
