Through The Bible Together

Week 1: Creation and the Fall

Old Testament Session
Kids (2nd-5th)

This Week's Focus

Learn about God's amazing creation, the first people He made, and what happened when sin came into the world. See how God always shows love and mercy even when people disobey.

Memory Verses

"In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth."

Genesis 1:1

"So God created man in His own image; in the image of God He created him; male and female He created them."

Genesis 1:27
Berean Standard Bible

Day 1: Creation

Today's Reading

Genesis 1:1 - 2:3

Think About It

  • What are some of your favorite things that God created? Why do you think God made so many different animals and plants?
  • What does it mean that people are made "in God's image"? How are we different from animals?
  • Why do you think God rested on the seventh day? What does this teach us about taking breaks?
  • When God looked at everything He made, He said it was "very good." How does this make you feel about the world around you?

Day 2: Adam and Eve

Today's Reading

Genesis 2:7-9, 15-25

Think About It

  • What job did God give Adam in the Garden of Eden? What are some ways kids can take care of God's creation?
  • Why did God make Eve to be Adam's helper? How do friends and family help each other?
  • Adam and Eve could talk to God and weren't afraid. How can we talk to God today?
  • Everything in the garden was perfect. What do you think that would have been like?

Day 3: The Fall

Today's Reading

Genesis 3:1-13, 20-24

Think About It

  • What trick did the serpent use to get Eve to disobey God? How do we sometimes get tricked into doing wrong things?
  • What happened when Adam and Eve disobeyed God? How did this change everything?
  • Even though Adam and Eve disobeyed, how did God show He still loved them?
  • When we do something wrong, do we sometimes want to hide like Adam and Eve did? What should we do instead?

Day 4: Cain and Abel

Today's Reading

Genesis 4:1-12

Think About It

  • Why do you think God was pleased with Abel's offering but not Cain's? What does this teach us about giving to God?
  • How did Cain feel when God didn't accept his offering? Have you ever felt jealous of someone else?
  • What does it mean to be your "brother's keeper"? How can we take care of our brothers, sisters, and friends?
  • Even after Cain did something terrible, God still protected him. What does this show us about God's love?

This Week's Big Ideas

Creation: God made everything beautiful and perfect.
Special Creation: People are made in God's image and are very special to Him.
Sin Changes Everything: When people disobey God, it breaks things and hurts relationships.
God's Love: Even when people do wrong things, God still loves them and wants to help them.
